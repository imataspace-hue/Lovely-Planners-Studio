Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as name, email address, or other details only when voluntarily provided by visitors through contact forms or direct email communication.

Log Files

Lovely Planners Studio follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any personally identifiable information.
