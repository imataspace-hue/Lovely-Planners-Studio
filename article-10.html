Why Teens With ADHD Struggle With Traditional Planning

  • Overcrowded pages cause overwhelm
  • Long to-do lists trigger anxiety
  • Lack of visual clarity reduces motivation
  • Rigid systems increase emotional pressure

ADHD minds need calm, simplicity, emotional safety — not pressure.

What Makes a Planner ADHD-Friendly?

  • Short daily task sections
  • Clear visual spacing
  • Emotional check-ins
  • Simple goal tracking
  • Positive reinforcement instead of punishment
